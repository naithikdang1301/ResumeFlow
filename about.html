👋 About Us

Our mission, our team, and why we built Resume Flow

Our Mission

Resume Flow was created with one simple goal: to help every student and fresher in India build a professional, job-ready resume — without any technical knowledge or design experience.

Every year, millions of engineering and college graduates enter the job market. Many lose great opportunities not because they lack skills, but because their resume doesn't represent them well or gets rejected by ATS systems before a human ever reads it.

We believe a great resume should be accessible to everyone — not just people who can afford professional resume writers or have design skills.

That's why we built a platform that is simple, completely free to start, and specifically designed for the Indian job market.

Why Does Your Resume Matter?

⏱️ 6 Seconds Rule

Studies show that recruiters spend only about 6 seconds scanning a resume before deciding whether to read further. First impressions matter enormously.

🤖 ATS Filters 75%

About 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS software before any human reads them. A well-formatted, keyword-optimized resume is essential today.

🏆 First Impression

Your resume is often the first thing an employer sees. A clean, professional resume shows you are organized, serious, and pay attention to detail.

🚪 Opens Doors

A great resume doesn't get you the job — but it gets you the interview. And an interview is your chance to show your real skills and personality.

What We Offer

📝

Easy Resume Builder
