❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the Free plan forever?
Yes! The Free plan never expires. You can create 1 resume and use 2 basic templates forever without paying anything.
Can I cancel my plan anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your Student or Pro plan at any time with no cancellation fees. Your plan stays active till the end of the billing period.
Is there a student discount?
Yes! The Student Plan is specially priced at just ₹99/month — keeping Indian college students in mind. You can also save 20% by choosing yearly billing.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and net banking. All payments are fully secure and encrypted. We use Razorpay for payment processing.
Are the templates really ATS-friendly?
Yes. All templates are tested against major ATS systems including Workday, Taleo, and Greenhouse. They use simple formatting and standard headings that every ATS can read correctly.
Can I download my resume as a PDF?
Yes. All plans — including the Free plan — allow PDF download. The Student and Pro plans additionally allow Word (.docx) download.
